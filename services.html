FAQ

Frequently asked questions:

Do you handle just design, or content / storytelling too?

Both. Our expertise is at the intersection of creative communications & business strategy. We not only help organize & design your pitch to captivate, but also offer insight & research to flesh out your business plan.

Storytelling is definitely a big part of what we do for decks: both in terms of communication & copy, and deeper questions of business context (e.g. market size, recent transactions, competitive landscape) and execution (e.g. go-to-market). While there are certainly some aspects that investors would be expected to come from your end (ie. roadmap, pricing), we're here to help fill in the gaps.

Our approach & understanding of go-to-market & financial strategy is informed by experience collaborating with investment firms & bankers, as well as being founders of our own ventures which have raised 7+ figures.

On the other hand, our skills as professional graphic designers brings a visual storytelling approach to concisely & effectively communicating your pitch.

How long does it take? How much does it cost?

Our process is quick & easy: as little as 10 business days to create your pitch deck (4-5 for the first draft, remainder for revisions) — though, we can deliver on rush timelines as needed (as quick as 3bd). For "Design + Research" projects, this timeline starts only after the proposed presentation outline is approved by the client (which usually takes just one day).

There are no surprise charges with revisions, as we always charge a flat fee, agreed upon before the start of work. For pricing details or to get a quote, get started here.

Who creates the deck?

An expert designer (with a succesful track record creating multi-million-dollar presentations) will be assigned to work with you 1-on-1 (via email). No outsourcing, no faceless team. Our experts do 100% of the work — from the graphic design, to the strategy/research, and will be in direct contact with you throughout the process.

What are the terms? Is there a contract?

You can read the full terms here. To briefly summarize:

  • Turnaround time: Standard turnaround time is 4-5 business days per draft. For "Design + Research" projects, this timeline starts only after the proposed presentation outline is approved by the client. If you need faster, ask us about rush delivery.
  • Confidentiality: we will keep your trade secrets confidential (we provide a pre-signed NDA)
  • No exclusivity: we are independent contractors & cannot accept any non-competes because we work with 100+ companies/yr.
  • Price tiers: pricing is based on # of slides — if you exceed the slide count that was originally paid for, you will be charged for the next tier up (+5 slides).
  • Presentation length: Total slide count is defined as all the unique slides created during the project — any appendices or slides that are replaced / removed during revisions will be included in the final editable PPT as hidden slides for your convenience and will count toward the total number of slides.
  • Cancellation: see full agreement for causes for termination (ie. no progress for 3 weeks or more) and terms (Clause 10)
  • Revisions: changes & feedback will be exchanged via annotations (ie. GDrive, Adobe Cloud), Loom, and email (no live working sessions or calls). If feedback is not received within 3 business days, the deliverables will be deemed accepted.
  • Payment: Invoices are due within 3 business days or receipt. Overdue payments will incur late invoice fees (Clause 3.1) and/or collection fees (Clause 3.5), per the agreement.
  • Portfolio: you grant us permission to showcase our work in portfolios, etc. (provided your prior written approval of the shared materials)
